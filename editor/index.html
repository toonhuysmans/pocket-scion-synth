Editor guide and signal flow

Signal flow

Sensor statistics shape three Euclidean lanes. Motif + scale + root create pitches, low articulation and an optional MIDI chord constrain them, then Bass, Pad, and Lead engines generate the audio.

Three synth tabs

Bass, Pad, and Lead are separate monophonic timbres. Dry Bass and Lead feed the Pad engine's Shared effects, so its chorus and delay process the complete mix.

Sequence and articulation

Scale, Motif, clock, Euclidean lengths, independent Bass/Pad/Lead density, gates, and ratchets define the composition. The low lane can be Bass, Percussion, Hybrid, or inherit the bank.

Saving and recovery

Knobs audition RAM immediately. Save patch, bank, or globals writes only that ownership scope. Revert loads the saved value; Restore removes the override and returns to the compiled default.

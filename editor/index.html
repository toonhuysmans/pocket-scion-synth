How low articulation and shared effects work

Low-lane mode

Inherit follows the bank. Bass stays pitched, Percussion articulates every low event, and Hybrid alternates bass and percussion while keeping strong anchors bass-led.

Six articulation slots

Each slot selects a drum sound, rhythmic role, weight, level, tune, tone, body/noise, decay, transient, and ratchet response. Weight zero disables a slot.

Musical selection

Anchor, backbeat, offbeat, fill, and free roles bias selection without creating a rigid pattern. The low Euclidean lane still decides when a hit occurs.

Sensor and effects

Sensor influence and Variation animate articulations and Hybrid balance. Chorus and delay under Pad + effects process bass, pad, and lead together and are saved per patch.
